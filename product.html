About Us

At Rural Handmade Products, the seeds of our journey were planted in a small village where tradition and craftsmanship were deeply rooted in the community. Our founder, Vivek Sharma, grew up surrounded by skilled artisans and farmers, witnessing the beauty and skill that goes into creating handcrafted goods and organic produce. He realized that these talented individuals deserved a broader audience — one that could truly appreciate their hard work and dedication.

In 2024, Vivek decided to take a leap of faith. Together with Anjali Patel, a passionate marketer with a vision for rural empowerment, and Rohit Gupta, a talented product designer, they began a mission to bridge the gap between rural artisans and global markets. They believed in giving these creators the platform they deserved, not only to showcase their work but to preserve their traditional skills and cultural heritage.

Vivek and his team started traveling from village to village, meeting with artisans and farmers, understanding their challenges, and gathering their stories. They were amazed at the wide variety of beautiful products — from handwoven textiles and pottery to organic spices and handmade crafts — each representing the unique spirit of its region.

With that vision in mind, Rural Handmade Products was born — an online marketplace that connects rural communities with people who value authenticity, quality, and sustainability. Every product sold through our platform directly benefits the artisans and farmers who create it, ensuring fair trade and sustainable growth for their communities.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to uplift rural communities by providing them with sustainable opportunities and global access. We aim to empower artisans and farmers to thrive while offering consumers a chance to experience the rich traditions and high-quality products from rural India.

Our Values

  • Empowerment: We believe in the power of creating opportunities that uplift rural communities.
  • Sustainability: Every product is made with care for the environment and for the livelihoods of the creators.
  • Fair Trade: We ensure that all artisans and farmers are paid fairly for their work, providing them with a dignified living.
